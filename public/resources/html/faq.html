What is Local Now?

Local now is a 24/7 on-demand video news stream for your community. You can count on customized local news and information based on where you live so it's relevant to you and your hectic life. We have everything you need before you head out the door: local news, weather and traffic updates. We provide a concise look at what's happening in the world. And we include the fun stuff, like what you should do this weekend: restaurants, festivals, events and great ways to stay active. All while keeping you on top of important weather coverage. It's the news that really matters and it's made just for you.

Why are you asking me for a location?

We produce hundreds of local streams across the country and we use your location to match you with the most local and relevant stream. Why isn't my location on the list of streams? When will my town be added? Sorry if we haven't made it to your location yet. We're in over 200 locations across the country and working hard to expand. Tell us where you live.

Why do some locations have more coverage than others?

Local Now's editorial team is creating as much video for as many locations in the U.S. as possible. We're on a mission to bring you the best coverage as we grow, but we have to start somewhere! So certain areas will have more stories and coverage than others. Tell us where you live.

I have a suggestion or idea for Local Now, who do I contact?

We love feedback and great ideas! Tell us what you think. Thank you!

How much does Local Now cost?

It's free, thanks for hanging out with us!

Does Local Now offer severe weather coverage?

The Weather Channel remains the trusted provider of all weather content on Local Now. During times of severe weather, The Weather Channel will be delivered in a unique stream to your location for the duration of the event, ensuring that you are safe and out of harm's way.

Why is The Weather Channel no longer available on Local Now?

Live streaming of The Weather Channel using your cable subscription has been removed from the new version of Local Now. This allows all users free access to Local Now.
